All about shrimp

What is shrimp?

Shrimp are small, aquatic animals belonging to the class Malacostraca and the order Decapoda. They are characterized by their elongated bodies and primarily swim by flexing their abdomen and tail. Shrimp are found in both freshwater and marine environments around the world.

List of different shrimps

  • White Shrimp (Litopenaeus setiferus)
  • Brown Shrimp (Farfantepenaeus aztecus)
  • Pink Shrimp (Farfantepenaeus duorarum)
  • Rock Shrimp (Sicyonia brevirostris)
  • Tiger Shrimp (Penaeus monodon)
